YOUR ADVENTURE AWAITS

007 has been targeted. MI6 needs you to investigate. Gather intelligence, navigate the city and solve clues in a bid to outsmart Spectre...

In collaboration with the producers of the James Bond films, 007: Shadow Of Spectre is a treasure hunt-style game which blends interactive gameplay with real-world exploration.

Your mission: become an MI6 agent, working with Moneypenny, Q Branch, and other characters to solve a trail of clues left by Bond.

To crack the case you must Read more >

To crack the case you must follow the clues sent to your mobile and embark on a journey across the city. You'll go undercover in secret locations and decipher clues as you solve the mystery of what happened to James.

Along your adventure you will uncover dead drops, engage with enemy associates, seek out private libraries, stalk through 17th century taverns, and sleuth through 5 star hotels.

Optional breaks allow aspiring agents to rest and rejuvenate at select locations during their mission, treating themselves to food and drink with exclusive discounts.

What you get

Go on a thrilling real-world adventure from £24.00 per player. Book early for the best prices. Children under 10 go free with adults and do not require a ticket.

Your experience includes:

  • An immersive experience across London

  • Interactive text messages and phone calls, for every ticketed player, with characters in the story

  • Hands-on physical puzzles for your team to solve

  • A story that changes with the decisions you make

  • Optional breaks in pubs and cafes

  • Interactions with people in the real world

PLAN YOUR EXPERIENCE

Play at your convenience, with fully flexible tickets.

When can I play?

The game runs every day, including bank holidays. Start your game between 10.00 and 19.30 Mon-Fri, 10.30 and 18.30 on Saturdays, and 12.05 and 16.30 on Sundays.

How long is the game?

3-4 hours across 2 miles, including optional breaks of up to 75 minutes.

Who can play?

Agents of all ages are welcome. Under-10s go free. Agents under-18 can play if there is an adult in the team, and you complete the game by 7pm, due to licensing restrictions - so start your game by 3pm to give yourself plenty of time. This game is dog-friendly including indoor locations 🐾.

Start your game at:The National Theatre, Upper Ground, London SE1 9PX
